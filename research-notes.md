## Artist Statement
> "Mother Nature, the ancestral knowledge of the ancient civilisations that lived in Peru, my home country, and the worldview of these civilisations inspire my work. The more I know about my ancestral culture, the more connected I feel to it, and I interpret it in my works from a very particular perspective."

> "An artwork becomes a complicity between the artist and the viewer, a kind of confessional."
